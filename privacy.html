This Privacy Policy describes how Top Shelf Picks ("we," "our," or "the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our application. By using Top Shelf Picks, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Top Shelf Picks collects the following information when you authorize our application:

  • OAuth Authentication Tokens: When you log in via social media platforms, Top Shelf Picks receives and stores OAuth tokens to authenticate API requests on your behalf.
  • Basic Account Information: Top Shelf Picks may access your username, display name, and profile information solely to identify your account for content publishing.
  • Published Content Data: Top Shelf Picks stores metadata about videos and content published through our application, including titles, descriptions, and posting timestamps.

2. How We Use Your Information

Top Shelf Picks uses the collected information exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Authenticating with social media platform APIs to publish video content on your behalf.
  • Scheduling and managing content posting to your authorized accounts.
  • Tracking published content performance within the application.

3. Data Storage and Security

Top Shelf Picks stores all authentication tokens and application data locally on your device or your designated server. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of stored API tokens and credentials.
  • Environment variable storage for sensitive keys (never hardcoded or committed to version control).
  • Access restricted to the authorized device or server running Top Shelf Picks.

4. Data Sharing

Top Shelf Picks does not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties. We only transmit data to the social media platform APIs (such as TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and Facebook) that you have explicitly authorized for content publishing.

5. Third-Party Services

Top Shelf Picks interacts with the following third-party services solely for the purposes described:

  • Social Media APIs: For publishing video content to your authorized accounts.
  • Google AI Services (Gemini, Veo): For generating video content and product research. No personal user data is sent to these services.
  • E-commerce APIs (Shopify, CJ Dropshipping, Trendsi): For product management and order fulfillment.

6. Data Retention

Top Shelf Picks retains your authentication tokens for as long as you use the application. You may revoke access at any time by:

  • Removing the application authorization from your social media account settings.
  • Deleting the locally stored credentials from your device.

7. Children's Privacy

Top Shelf Picks is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
